Our Initiatives

2023

February to August - Weekly Interaction Sessions
A platform for volunteers to interact with people with Dementia and forge greater bonds.

Curae Shares
Sharing information about Dementia with the public through social media!

Info-Cards
Cards with information about Dementia to be disseminated during Curae Delivers and Project Curae Seminar!

21 March - Service Learning Festival
Fun-filled activities for students to learn more about Dementia and the struggles Dementia patients face!

31 March - Curae Delivers
Dissemination of information cards about Dementia together with food sales to our school community!

17 June - Project Curae Seminar
Letting the public better understand the plights of people with dementia and empathise with them and their caregivers! Info-cards given out too!

21 July - Racial Harmony Day Bazaar
Increasing outreach and raising awareness regarding Dementia while celebrating Racial Harmony Day!

28 July - Hwa Chong-Nanyang Combined Sports Meet Booth
Increasing our outreach and raising awareness regarding dementia through sports and games!
