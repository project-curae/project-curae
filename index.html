About Us

Who are we?

We are Project Curae 4.0, a service learning project comprising of 5 secondary 4 students from the High School Section of Hwa Chong Institution, Singapore.

Through working with Dementia Singapore, we hope to raise awareness about dementia patients in Singapore and improve their lives. Our main goals are raising awareness for people with Dementia and attaining a better understanding of them through interactive sessions.

Underlying Problem

Given that the general public is unaware of the stigmatization with the lack of awareness that Dementia patients face, how can we ensure that the voices of people with Dementia, especially those who feel lonely and rejected, are heard, and encourage Singaporeans, especially youths, to develop empathy for them and eliminate public misconceptions that people may have of persons with Dementia so that they can live in a caring and Dementia-inclusive society as well as have a higher quality of life in 2023 and beyond?
